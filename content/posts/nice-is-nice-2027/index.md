We are in Nice now and have been for quite some time.  We arrived this morning and quickly found our hotel. We are getting good at reading maps and finding our way around new towns.  The hotel is all right. It is fairly run-down, but it is a place to sleep, so I can't complain.  I have spent most of the afternoon sleeping and recovering from several days of very little rest. Soon I am going to walk around with Sibyl and Ash, visit a Russian cathedral, and find some pastries.  Speaking of pastries, I met an attractive Hungarian woman named Anna on the train.  She invited me to stay at her flat in Paris, although I already had a place to stay.  In hindsight, it was early and I wasn't quite thinking.  Hopefully I will meet more people like her on my travels who will let me stay with them.  For now, I'm off to walk around!

My regular weekends involve French on Saturday and lounging around on Sunday. This Sunday, however, we had other plans. My friends Daisy and Remko invited me over for a few drinks and a game of boules. The score was initially 2-12, and I came back to lead 14-12. Remko and Daisy kept tossing the jack short, and eventually pulled significantly ahead. We then moved on to an excellent dinner: Indonesian rice, cabbage, plenty of beer, and some chicken. Some great chicken.

Daisy regularly throws pots for fun and asked me to take a few home. I selected two small bowls, which have become my yoghurt bowls. I used to throw pots when I was younger, and I have to say these are of excellent quality. Much appreciated!

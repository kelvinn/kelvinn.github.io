I have several months of back entries to catch up on, but I find the way my "household" operates quite funny: there are three of us, and we sit in an MSN chat room. I used to do something similar when I lived in a huge house in Portland, but it is especially amusing in our two-bedroom apartment. I could even tap out Morse code to my flatmate right now.

In other news, I've just upgraded the router and called TPG, our ISP, to finish upgrading our DSL to ADSL2+. We are stable at 16 Mbps down and 1 Mbps up, which is just fine for me. I don't download much anyway, but this makes SVN so much quicker.

For now, I'm working on a web app with my friend Sean, so I had better get back to it. I only have about two hours of "Kelvin time" each day, and those two hours are nearly up.

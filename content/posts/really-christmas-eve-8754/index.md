I'm at an internet cafe, and it is roasting in here! I'm in London right now, after a pretty rough flight. We left Berlin late and, after a second taxi attempt on arrival, missed the last train. We then took several buses to our hostel, arriving at about 3 a.m. I collapsed into bed, woke at 9:10, had breakfast, and packed a lunch too. Since 10:00, I have been walking around and have seen almost everything in the central area, which was satisfying. I took a 15-minute bus ride without knowing where it was going (a little mission Casey sent me on before I left him this morning). I do not know how there are not more bus accidents here; the drivers are chaotic! I got on, he took one turn and I swear I heard him laugh as I got flung across the bus (I was alone on the upper deck). A sharp corner, a clipped kerb, and Kelvin went flying; you get the idea. Food calls, so I'm going to check a few final things and see what Earl's Court supermarkets have to offer! P.S. We are still working out where we will sleep tonight...

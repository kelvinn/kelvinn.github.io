Not much happened, so I decided to catch a train somewhere. I did not really know where, and did not particularly care. I boarded a train to La Spezia, or somewhere like it, then changed trains and continued beyond Pisa. The funniest part was seeing Jenn at a station from the train when it stopped. Ha! She looked great, as usual. Now I am on another train heading somewhere south. I still do not know where, and still do not mind. I am in a comfortable cabin with several people, and nobody is asking me for money. As I did two nights ago, I plan to sleep on the beach, although tonight's destination does not have one. What shall I do? I will get some food when I leave the train, and everything will become clear. Until later, my friend. Good night, and sleep well.

In the morning, I packed and headed back to Peggy's.

I snacked throughout the day while Peggy baked banana bread. That night, my companion, Peggy's family, and I went to a steak restaurant and enjoyed a generous meal. I was slightly too full by the end, but that is part of a big dinner.

Later, I visited a night market held in a large parking lot and sampled a little more food. Once I became tired, I walked back to the car, returned to Peggy's house, and quickly fell asleep. The next day, I would say goodbye and head back to Taipei with plenty of memories.

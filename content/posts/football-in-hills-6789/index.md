Today we wandered through the hills above Santa Barbara, exploring a maze of valleys before reaching the top of one. As we looked over the city, we talked about the "good old days."

A concrete pillar stood nearby. After climbing onto it, Ben said, "I bet I could throw the football to Pav." Both Ben and Pav had played football in high school, while I had been learning how to use Red Hat Linux 5.2.

Without further ado, here is the throw. I was behind the camera, naturally.
